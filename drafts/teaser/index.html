News from the developers

Ready to roll!

At Mon, 2/2/2015 by icharala

Hello there! After a long year of development, the game has reached it's first milestone! We have completed all the little bits and piecies and the integration with the scientific software. Regarding the software development, the game is ready to roll out!

After we have completed the education material and the scientific content, in a matter of weeks, we will send an e-mail to all of you who have registered for the early public alpha. In the e-mail you will find a username and password that you can use in order to test it!

We are expecting your feedback in order to fix all the rough edges and to provide all the missing descriptions that will make Virtual Atom Smasher entertaining and educational!
